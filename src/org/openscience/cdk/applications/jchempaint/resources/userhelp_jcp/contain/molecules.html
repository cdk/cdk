Creating Molecules

Start a new document, by clicking the white paper with the yellow star on the top left corner. In the Draw mode (the bond button), click and hold the mouse at any place and drag the mouse in any direction. Release the mouse button when the bond is pointing in the wanted direction.

Enter the Symbol mode, and click one of the atoms. This will rotate the atom symbol through a small list of commom elements.
